<i>Musify</i> is an app for streaming and downloading music. Its features include:

* Online Song Search
* Streaming Support
* Download Support
* Play Local / Downloaded Songs Supported
* High Quality m4a / mp3 / flac Format
* Lyrics Support
* No Ads
* No Subscriptions
* SponsorBlock Support
* 12 Supported Languages
* Material UI & Accent Colors
